<p>This map shows <strong>34</strong> U-boats lost in the North Sea during WWII.
Two U-boats went missing in the North Sea during the war; <a href="/boats/u54.htm">U-54</a> (KrvKpt. <a href="/men/commanders/688.html">Günter Kutschmann</a>) in 1940 and <a href="/boats/u702.htm">U-702</a> (KrvKpt. <a href="/men/commanders/959.html">Wolf-Rüdiger von Rabenau</a>) in 1942. Both are shown on the map.</p>

<p>In most sources <a href="/boats/u102.htm">U-102</a> is listed as missing in the North Sea but
she was lost southwest of Ireland on 1 July, 1940.</p>
