	<h1>The English Channel</h1>
</div>


<p>The first U-boat lost in the Channel were the <a href="/boats/u12.htm">U-12</a>, <a href="/boats/u16.htm">U-16</a> and <a href="/boats/u40.htm">U-40</a> in 1939. From then on U-boats avoided the Channel for the most part due to fear of large allied minefields there.</p>

<p>The last boats to be sunk here were 
<a href="/boats/u1063.htm">U-1063</a>, <a href="/boats/u1195.htm">U-1195</a>,
<a href="/boats/u275.htm">U-275</a> and <a href="/boats/u772.htm">U-772</a> in Dec 44 - April 1945.</p>
