	<h1>The Mediterranean</h1>
</div>




<p>The first boats to enter the area were the <a href="/boats/u97.htm">U-97</a> and <a href="/boats/u559.htm">U-559</a> on 26 Sept, 1941. Any U-boat that entered the Mediterranean was lost forever to the Battle of the Atlantic as the currents in the <a href="gibraltar.htm">Straits of Gibraltar</a> were simply too strong to escape that way again while submerged (the only feasible way due to strong Allied ASW patrols in the Straits).</p>


<p>In this body of water the allies successfully tried a new ASW tactic called <strong>Swamp</strong>, see file on <a href="/boats/u371.htm">U-371</a> (Oblt. <a href="/men/fenski.htm">Horst-Arno Fenski</a>) for more info.</p>
