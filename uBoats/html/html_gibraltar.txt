	<h1>The Straits of Gibraltar</h1>
</div>

<p>This is the area through which all boats entering the <a href="/ops/mediterranean.htm">Mediterranean</a> had to traverse. The Straits of Gibraltar itself was a very dangerous part of the journey as it is very narrow and was always heavily patroled by allied vessels. U-boats did patrol these waters as they were used heavily for shipping, among others to sustain the war in Africa.</p>
