	<h1>The Black Sea</h1>
</div>

<p>This was the patrol area of the <a href="/flotillas/30flo.htm">30th Flotilla</a> at Constanza from Oct 1942 to Oct 1944. All its 6 U-boats were transported via land to the Black Sea and all of them were lost there. 3 were scuttled by their crews near Eregli, Turkey on 10 Sept 1944 to escape capture by the Russians.</p>
