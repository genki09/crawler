	<h1>The Barents Sea</h1>
</div>

<p>One of the biggest convoy battles happened here in 1942 when <a href="/ops/convoys/pq-17.htm">PQ-17</a> was almost eliminated. The boats operating there were mostly from the <a href="/flotillas/11flo.htm">11th Flotilla</a> in Bergen and the <a href="/flotillas/13flo.htm">13th Flotilla</a> in Trondheim.</p>

<p>This area saw the battles for convoys PQ, QP, RA and JW from 1942 to 1945. The path shown in red on the map is the approximate route for these convoys.</p>
