	<h1>The Baltic</h1>
</div>

<p>The losses around the Bay of Danzig (Gdansk) were mostly due to very heavy mining operations by the British in 1944.
    This helped delay the deployment of the <a href="/types/xxi.htm">XXI boats</a> in late 1944.</p>

<p>This map does not include the more than 200 U-boats <a href="/fates/scuttled.htm">scuttled</a> there in May 1945.</p>
