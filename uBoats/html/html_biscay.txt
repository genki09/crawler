	<h1>The Bay of Biscay</h1>
</div>

<p>This body of water was known as the <strong>Valley of Death</strong> among U-boat men from 1943 onwards. At that time the RAF had gained total air supremacy all over the bay and was sinking U-boats only few hours from their bunkers. The failure of the <strong>Luftwaffe</strong> to protect the area is baffling.</p>
