	<h1>Kattegat / Skagerrak</h1>
	<!--<p><img src="/media/maps/kattegat.gif" width="402" height="410" alt="" usemap="#kattegat" /></p>-->
</div>
	


<p>Through these confined waters U-boats traveling to their combat flotillas in France or Norway had to sail through. Only 1 U-boat was lost here prior to 1944; <a href="/boats/u22.htm">U-22</a> (Kptlt. <a href="/men/commanders/550.html">Karl-Heinrich Jenisch</a>) went missing in late March 1940 in North Sea or Kattegat and is not shown on this map, but she is included in the figures lost.</p>

<p>All the boats lost here fell victim to aircraft, either by direct attack or air laid mines. At the southern end of this area some of the roughly 220 U-boats were <a href="/fates/scuttled.htm">scuttled in May 1945</a>.</p>
