	<h1>Cape Farewell</h1>
</div>

<p>This area saw some of the biggest <a href="/ops/convoys/battles.htm">convoy battles</a> of the U-boat war, several convoy routes passed right through this area, or just to the southwest of it.</p>

<p>Of special fates here are <a href="/boats/u110.htm">U-110</a> that was captured in May 1941. Its capture was of immense value for
the allies. The <a href="/boats/u254.htm">U-254</a> was rammed and sunk by its sister ship, <a href="/boats/u221.htm">U-221</a> (Trojer).</p>
